Design Studio
 
Apparel Studio
 
At the heart of our brand is a deep commitment to storytelling through prints and garment designs. The journey of transforming an idea into a creative print or a tangible garment is dynamic. Each design is thoughtfully crafted, blending imagination with technical precision. Prints are central to our brand language. We sketch, refine and digitalize motifs, experimenting with patterns and colourways. Simultaneously, we design garments with overall concept. Technical drawings, pattern development and sourcing are handled to ensure both form and function. Our collections are presented through curated lookbooks that reflect the essence of the process- from idea, to sketch, to creation.
